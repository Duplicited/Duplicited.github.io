Duckenomics

Taxes

Each buy and sell from Uniswap will have a tax attached to them. Buys are taxes at a very nice 4.2069% while sells are taxed at an also nice 8.008135%. The tokens from the taxes are added onto the contract’s balance.

Funding and Liquidity

Once the contract’s balance has reached 1 million (1% wallet) tokens, the contract will sell. A set percentage of this sell will be distributed in ETH to the community wallet. The rest will be automatically added in even parts token/ETH to our burned liquidity duck pool.

Liquidity Locked Forever

All LP tokens were burnt on launch. This means there is zero chance of a rugpull as it is impossible to pull liquidity!
